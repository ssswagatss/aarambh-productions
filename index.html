Our Story

We never
just document.
We feel.

Aarambh — the beginning — was born from a single conviction: that the best photographs are the ones you feel before you see.

Founded in 2018, we are a team of visual storytellers who believe that every ceremony, every glance, every tear, and every laugh holds an entire universe inside it. Our job is simply to find it.

From the backstreets of Jaipur's old city to palace courtyards in Udaipur and rooftop ceremonies in Mumbai — we have followed love across 12 cities, hundreds of stories, and thousands of frames that will outlast us all.

— Rohan Verma, Founder & Lead Cinematographer
